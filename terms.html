The service

You're Not Alone lets you write a private, anonymous confession and shows you one past confession that feels similar, with a count of how many people felt the same. There is intentionally no messaging, no replies, and no way to contact another user. Do not attempt to identify or reach anyone.

Eligibility — adults only

You must be 18 or older. Providing a false date of birth is a violation and grounds for termination.

Your content

You keep ownership of what you write. You grant us a licence to store, run automated safety analysis on, match, and display your confession anonymously to other users, and to include it in shareable images — solely to operate the service. Don't include information that identifies you or anyone else.

Prohibited

  • Anything that sexualises or endangers a minor (detected, blocked, and reported — zero tolerance).
  • Threats, harassment, doxxing, or targeting a real person.
  • Hate, incitement or glorification of violence, or illegal content.
  • Spam, automation, or attempts to bypass the safety pipeline.

Every submission passes automated moderation before it is stored. Repeated violations lead to temporary and then permanent bans.

Not a crisis service

If your submission suggests you may be at risk, we show support resources instead of publishing it. We are not a crisis, medical, or emergency service and do not monitor submissions in real time. If you are in danger, contact local emergency services.

Subscriptions

Optional supporter subscriptions are billed through the App Store / Google Play and renew until cancelled. Manage or cancel anytime in your store account. Crisis resources are always free and never behind a plan.

Termination & changes

We may suspend accounts for violations or as required by law. You may delete your account anytime. We'll notify you of material changes to these terms. Contact nani.ajay@gmail.com.
