Content Policy

Last updated 16 June 2026

This is a place to say the things you can't say out loud — anonymously, and safely. Every confession passes automated safety checks before it is stored or shown to anyone, and humans review what gets reported.

What's never allowed

  • Anything that sexualises or endangers a minor. Detected, blocked, and reported to the authorities. Zero tolerance.
  • Targeting real people — no names, doxxing, threats, or harassment.
  • Hate — content that attacks people for who they are.
  • Violence — threats, incitement, or glorification.
  • Illegal content or solicitation of illegal activity.
  • Spam and automated submissions.

How enforcement works

Before anything is stored, automated moderation screens every submission; violating content is rejected and never enters the space. If a submission suggests someone may be at risk, we show support resources instead of publishing it. Every confession you're shown has a one-tap report control; reported content is hidden immediately and reviewed by a human. Violations lead to escalating bans at the account and device level.

What we built on purpose

There are no replies, messages, profiles, or followers — so no one can use this app to reach, pressure, or identify you. That absence is the safety feature.

Appeals & grievances

If you believe content was wrongly removed, contact nani.ajay@gmail.com. We acknowledge reports promptly and act on them as fast as we can.
