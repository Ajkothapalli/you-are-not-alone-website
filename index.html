built safe, on purpose

the absence of a reply channel is the design

We removed everything that lets people hurt each other — then built around what's left.

Never tied to you

Your confession is split from your identity at the source. Even we can't connect it back to you.

No replies, profiles, or DMs

There's no way for anyone to message, follow, or target you. The silence is the safety.

Checked before it's shown

Automated safety review runs on every post before it can ever be matched to another person.

Support, never a sale

If you're in crisis you get resources, never a paywall. Relief is always free — that line never moves.
